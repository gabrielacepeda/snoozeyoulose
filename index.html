How it works

After your alarm goes off, you have ten minutes to shut if off. If you don't hit it off on time, we will send out a tweet, email, and text to your specified recipients with your specified message. We suggest making your messages as productive or embarrasing as possible to incentivize actually getting up. Yes, we know this will work better as an app. We're working on it.
